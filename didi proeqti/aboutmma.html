GOA Martial Arts refers to a training program or academy
focused on teaching self-defense and combat sports skills.
Like many martial arts schools, it usually combines physical
training with discipline, respect, and mental development.



Training often includes:


  • Basic striking techniques (punches, kicks, blocks)
  • Self-defense movements and reactions
  • Fitness and endurance exercises
  • Flexibility and coordination drills
  • Sparring practice (controlled fighting)
  • Discipline and self-control
  • Confidence building
  • Focus and mental strength
  • Respect for instructors and training partners
